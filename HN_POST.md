## Expected Comments

Likely questions (prepare short answers):

**"Why synthetic data, not real EEG?"**
→ Real data is the next step. Synthetic validates the theory, then we measure on actual brains.

**"Have you tested on garden-path sentences?"**
→ Ambiguous versions sometimes show lower Δλ, sometimes not (depends on parser). It's realistic—the effect isn't perfect.

**"Isn't this just surprisal theory with extra steps?"**
→ Different mechanism. Surprisal = statistical prediction error. Spectral gap = structural clarity. Both predict frequency, different reasons.

**"How does this scale to long documents?"**
→ Per-sentence analysis. Long documents = multiple oscillations. Could track frequency evolution through text.

**"Can you use this for machine translation?"**
→ Yes, that's a potential application. Haven't tested yet.
